Flynn & the Tractor Thief

A charmingly illustrated storybook I created for my son, inspired by his love for adventure and discovery. The illustrations were hand-drawn using the Affinity suite on a tablet, allowing for a blend of digital precision and traditional storytelling warmth. Every page is filled with vibrant characters and engaging details designed to captivate young imaginations. This personal project was not only a creative challenge but also a heartfelt way to bring a unique, one-of-a-kind tale to life for my Son."
